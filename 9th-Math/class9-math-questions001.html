Class 9 Mathematics – English Questions Only

Chapter 1: Number System

  • Write the decimal expansion of 1/7. Is it terminating or non-terminating?
  • Represent √5 on the number line geometrically.
  • Rationalize the denominator of 1/(√7 + √6).
  • Find two rational numbers between √2 and √3.
  • Express 0.333... as a rational number.
  • Is π a rational or irrational number? Justify your answer.

Chapter 2: Polynomials

  • Find the degree of the polynomial 5x³ - 2x² + x - 7.
  • If p(x) = x² - 3x + 2, find p(1), p(2), and p(0).
  • Factorize x² - 5x + 6.
  • Show that x = 2 is a zero of the polynomial x² - 4x + 4.
  • Divide x³ + 3x² + 3x + 1 by x + 1.

Chapter 3: Linear Equations in Two Variables

  • Write the standard form of a linear equation in two variables.
  • Find two solutions of the equation 2x + 3y = 6.
  • Draw the graph of the equation x + y = 4.
  • If x = 1, find the value of y in the equation 3x + 2y = 7.
  • Write a linear equation whose graph passes through the points (0, 2) and (2, 0).

Chapter 4: Lines and Angles

  • Define complementary angles with an example.
  • Prove that if two lines intersect, the vertically opposite angles are equal.
  • If ∠A + ∠B = 180°, what type of angles are they?
  • State the angle sum property of a triangle.
  • Prove that alternate interior angles are equal when a transversal intersects two parallel lines.

Chapter 5: Triangles

  • State and prove the Pythagoras Theorem.
  • Define congruent triangles.
  • Prove that in a triangle, angles opposite to equal sides are equal.
  • State SAS congruence criterion.
  • In triangle ABC, AB = AC and angle B = 40°, find angle C.

Chapter 6: Coordinate Geometry

  • Write the coordinates of the origin.
  • Plot the point (3, -2) on the Cartesian plane.
  • What is the x-coordinate of the point (0, 5)?
  • Find the distance between the points (2, 3) and (2, -1).
  • Write the coordinates of a point on the x-axis.

Chapter 7: Statistics

  • Define mean of a data set.
  • Find the median of the data: 3, 5, 7, 9, 11.
  • Calculate the mode of the data: 2, 4, 4, 6, 8.
  • Draw a bar graph for the following data: Apples – 10, Bananas – 15, Oranges – 5.
  • What is the range of the data: 12, 18, 25, 30?

Chapter 8: Geometry (Quadrilaterals & Circles)

  • Define a parallelogram and list its properties.
  • Prove that the diagonals of a rectangle are equal.
  • What is a cyclic quadrilateral?
  • Prove: The angle subtended by a diameter is a right angle.
  • In a circle, prove that equal chords are equidistant from the center.

Chapter 9: Mensuration

  • Find the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 6 cm.
  • Calculate the surface area of a cube of side 5 cm.
  • Find the volume of a cylinder with radius 7 cm and height 10 cm.
  • A cone has height 12 cm and radius 5 cm. Find its volume.
  • Find the total surface area of a sphere of radius 4 cm.

Chapter 10: Probability

  • Define probability.
  • A coin is tossed once. What is the probability of getting a head?
  • A die is thrown. Find the probability of getting an even number.
  • What is the probability of drawing a red card from a deck of 52 cards?
  • If a number is chosen at random from 1 to 10, what is the probability that it is a prime?
